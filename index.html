About Country Farm Matugga

Uganda's Leading Poultry Supplier

Country Farm Matugga is Uganda's premier supplier of high-quality day-old chicks and poultry products. With over 15 years of experience, we've built a reputation for excellence in breeding, health, and customer service.

Our state-of-the-art hatchery ensures that every chick meets the highest standards of health and genetics. We work directly with farmers across Uganda to support sustainable and profitable poultry farming.

From small backyard farms to large commercial operations, we provide the foundation for your poultry success.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age are the chicks when delivered?

Our day-old chicks are delivered within 24-48 hours of hatching, ensuring they're at the optimal age for transport and acclimation to your farm.

Do you offer vaccination services?

Yes! All our chicks come pre-vaccinated against Marek's disease. We also offer additional vaccination services upon request.

What's your minimum order quantity?

We accept orders starting from 50 chicks for small-scale farmers. However, orders above 100 chicks qualify for free delivery within the Kampala area.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Kampala takes 1-2 days. For upcountry deliveries, we coordinate with you to ensure safe and timely delivery.
