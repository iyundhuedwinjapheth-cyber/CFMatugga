About Country Farm Matugga

Uganda's Leading Poultry Supplier

Country Farm Matugga is Uganda's premier supplier of high-quality day-old chicks and poultry products. With over 15 years of experience, we've built a reputation for excellence in breeding, health, and customer service.

Our state-of-the-art hatchery ensures that every chick meets the highest standards of health and genetics. We work directly with farmers across Uganda to support sustainable and profitable poultry farming.

From small backyard farms to large commercial operations, we provide the foundation for your poultry success.
